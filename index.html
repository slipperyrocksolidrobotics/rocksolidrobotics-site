Our Mission

Rock Solid Robotics is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to bringing world-class STEM education to students in the Slippery Rock Area School District. Through hands-on robotics instruction, we equip young minds with the skills, confidence, and curiosity to thrive in tomorrow’s technology-driven world.

  • Engineering and coding fundamentals
  • Teamwork and problem-solving
  • Real-world applications of science and technology
  • Character development through competition and collaboration

Our Vision

We’re not just launching a robotics team — we’re building a movement. Our long-term goal is to create a vertically integrated robotics program that supports students from their first LEGO build to advanced high school competitions. With your support, we can:

  • Expand our offerings to include FTC and FRC programs
  • Provide scholarships and equipment for underserved students
  • Host community STEM events and workshops
  • Partner with local educators and industry leaders

How You Can Help

Your donation directly supports student access to robotics kits, competition fees, mentorship, and more. As a 501(c)(3), all contributions are tax-deductible and go toward building a stronger, smarter, and more resilient Slippery Rock community.
