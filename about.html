About Rock Solid Robotics

Rock Solid Robotics is an all-volunteer 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to advancing STEM education through robotics programs for students in the Slippery Rock Area School District and beyond.

Commitment to Safety

We comply with FIRST’s Youth Protection Program, including Youth Protection Screenings for Lead Mentors, to ensure a safe and supportive environment for all participants.
