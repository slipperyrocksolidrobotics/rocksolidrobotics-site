Who We Are

Rock Solid Robotics is an all-volunteer 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to advancing education in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics for students in the Slippery Rock Area School District and beyond.

We comply with FIRST’s Youth Protection Program, including Youth Protection Screenings for all Lead Mentors.
